    History about Farm.

    The journey began when Baburao Balasaheb completed his 25 years of service in the Indian Army, but for him, retirement was not an option. He wanted to continue to serve the motherland, and it was this motivation that made him start organic farming in 2014, even before organic farming was popular.

    He always believed that organic farming used to be just farming for our ancestors but as agriculture became commercialized fertilized foods became common. He is always against this practice and strongly desires to make organic fruits and vegetables easily accessible and affordable to everyone.

    He is living for a simple but important statement of ‘Making organic living effortless’.

    Starting from a small piece of land, Shantai’s organic farms have now grown over 11 acres of 100% natural organic fruits and vegetables and have also become a popular tourist gateway to Purandar’s natural beauty.
